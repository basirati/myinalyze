Eyes On The Market is designed to provide the Product Owner and the team with the current word on the market. First it identifies the yearly trends specified by market researchers, then crawls different sources such as Twitter, TechCrunch, and Google Index, and finds out how often each of those topics is discussed about on the market. After aggregating the result from different sources, it visualizes the insights using bubbles. Each bubble represents a trending topic in real-time. The size of the bubbles increases once the identified topic is being more popular and shrinks once the hype around the topic fades. This helps the Product Owner have a general idea about what the market is talking about, and use the insights in sprint planning, backlog prioritization and strategy setting.

Click the Trends on the menu bar to view the current trends.
