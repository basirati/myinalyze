Eyes On The Market is designed to provide the Product Owner and the team with the current word on the market. It first identifies the trending topics, then crawls the web and finds out how often each of those topics is discussed about. Then it provides the Product Owner with bubbles, each representing a trending topic in real-time. The size of the bubbles increases once the identified topic is being more popular and shrinks once the hype around the topic fades. This helps the PO have a general idea about what the market is talking about.

Click the 'Trends' on the menu bar to view the current trends.
