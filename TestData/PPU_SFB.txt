Emergency stop button must be provided at multiple locations that should be easily accessible to the operators.
The PPU components must not be manufactured by any toxic substance.
An emergency stop button must be integrated with PPU to halt the operations safely without causing any damage to the system in case of emergency.
An efficient transportation mechanism could be used to transport different work pieces fastly.
The sorting unit, stamping unit and stack must be capable of handling all kinds of work pieces.
PPU should be able to measure the weight of the work pieces and distinguish different work pieces based on it.
Any component or live wire without recommended insulation carrying current above safety limits should not be exposed to the outside environment.
The time to handle stamp and sort all of the work pieces should be 10 minutes.
